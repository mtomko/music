# CAPRIOL

This part is a combination of the double bass part and the lower divisi of the cello part, as orchestrated by J. Ramón Hernández.
The bass part contains a number of long stretches of rests in the Pavane and Mattachins where I have copied in the lower cello line.
I have not marked which notes come from the cello part and which come from the bass, however, because using a cue voice adds some complexity and causes lilypond to render rests for the main part. 
Since I intend to play what would be the cello cues, it's simpler to just note the lines equally.
Likewise, I have chosen octaves based on my judgement; in certain passages the bass will play the cello line an octave down, in others at the same pitch.
